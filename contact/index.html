Pitch and Clubhouse

Our club house - Ruck 'n Maul - is located at Triomflaan 52, 1160 Auderghem right across the street. There you will find live rugby, good food, free wifi, a pool table, karaoke & a friendly atmosphere. It is run by rugby players for rugby & sports enthusiasts. You can check more info about it here.
