Introduction

Founded in 1995, the BBRFC-Celtic Women have been a consistent presence in Belgian Womenâ€™s Rugby. Highlights throughout their history include winning the Triple Crown in 2005-2006 and becoming the Belgian Champions.

We are proud to call ourselves a through-and-through international team that today's team includes over a dozen nationalities. We are open to players from all backgrounds and levels of experience, and offer an excellent networking opportunity (read social side). Club socials, pubcrawls, BBQ's... lift the team spirit and give members a great opportunity to socialise and network with people from all over the world.

When and Where

Practice sessions are held every Wednesday from 20:00 - 22:00 and Friday from 19:00 - 21:00 at the VUB rugby pitch, found in front of the tram stop VUB.

Joining

Become a part of a multi-national, multicultural team and experience the fun of playing rugby! If you are interested, and would like to know more, feel free to contact us at ladiesxv@bbrfc-celtic.be.

The first 3 training sessions are entirely free - what a bargain! After that or before your first match you will need to hand in your registration and medical forms for insurance purposes, and of course pay your registration fees.

Payment policy!

We have a strict policy regarding the fee payment. No player is to play or be registered until the fee has been paid. The latter is considered outstanding if not settled by the end of September or 30 days after having joined. After that, a €10 increase will be applied.

Payment plans are always an option, so please contact our treasurer if you would like one.

VUB students who wish to only train with us can do so for free, without filling in any forms, provided they are insured by the VUB. To play matches, the normal procedure applies.
