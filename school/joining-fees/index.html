Joining

If you would like to give us a try just send us an email (rugbyschool@bbrfc-celtic.be) so that we can let you know when is the next training session. All you need to try out is a pair of rugby boots or trainers, shorts and a T-Shirt or jersey. Note that because we play on a high tech artificial pitch only plastic studs may be used (no metal studs)
.

The first three training sessions are free, so why not drop in & give us a go? Chances are you'll be back......

Registration

In order to register you need to send us a scanned copy of the medical form (see above) and an email (at rugbyschool@bbrfc-celtic.be) with the following information:

  • First name
  • Family name
  • Gender
  • Nationality
  • Date and place of birth
  • Address
  • Phone number
  • Email address

Medical Form

This is partially filled out by yourself and the bottom left of it is completed by your doctor who must sign and stamp the part he/she completes. The medical examination should cost you less than 30â‚¬ and take 5-10 minutes maximum!

Once you have the completed medical form please send us a good scanned copy by email. We will get you a license. The license entitles you to FBRB insurance AND authorizes you to play in all FBRB sanctioned matches.

Payment policy!

We have a strict policy regarding the fee payment. No player is to play or be registered until the fee has been paid. The latter is considered outstanding if not settled by the end of September. After that, a €10 increase will be applied.

Payment plans are always an option, so please contact our treasurer if you would like one.
